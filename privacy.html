Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Diffuria AI ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

1. Data We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple User ID and, if you choose to share it, your name and email address. We use this solely for authentication and account management.

Generated Content: Images and videos you create are stored locally on your device. When generating content, your text prompts and uploaded images are sent to our servers for AI processing and are not stored after generation is complete.

Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data (features used, generation counts, app sessions) to improve the app experience. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Purchase Information: Subscription and purchase data is managed by Apple and our payment partner (Adapty). We receive transaction confirmations but never see your payment details.

Device Tokens: If you enable notifications, we store your device push notification token to send you generation completion alerts.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and manage your subscription
  • To process your AI generation requests
  • To track your credit balance and usage limits
  • To send push notifications about completed generations
  • To improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • AI Model Providers (fal.ai): Your prompts and uploaded images are sent for processing. They are not stored after generation.
  • Adapty: Subscription management and purchase validation.
  • Supabase: Backend database for account data, hosted in the US with encryption at rest.

4. Data Storage & Security

Generated images and videos are stored only on your device. Account data is stored securely in our cloud database with encryption. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's Keychain.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained while your account is active. You can delete all app data from Settings within the app. Generated content is stored locally and deleted when you clear your history or delete the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Diffuria AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

  • Delete all your data from within the app (Profile → Delete All Data)
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Opt out of analytics by disabling app tracking in iOS Settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or by updating the date above.

9. Contact

Questions? Visit our support page.
