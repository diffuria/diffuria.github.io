Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Diffuria AI ("we", "our", or "the app") is an AI image and video creation service. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and what controls you have.

1. Information We Collect

Device-based account information: The app currently uses a device-based identifier rather than a traditional username/password account. We store an anonymous account ID, authentication tokens, and security-related identifiers such as App Attest data when available.

Generation inputs and media: When you create content, we may collect prompts, negative prompts, uploaded images, masks, reference videos, and audio files you choose to submit for processing.

Generation history and service records: We store operational records such as prompt text, model used, credit cost, generation status, timestamps, and output URLs so the app can show history, track credits, troubleshoot failures, and operate the service.

Purchase and entitlement data: We receive transaction confirmations, product identifiers, subscription status, and related metadata from Apple and our subscription provider. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Usage analytics: We collect app events such as screen views, generation attempts, credit changes, session data, and similar product analytics tied to your anonymous account ID.

Notification data: If you allow notifications, we store your device push token to send generation and app-related alerts.

2. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate your device-based account and keep you signed in
  • Process image, video, editing, and lip-sync requests
  • Track credit balance, purchases, subscriptions, refunds, and usage limits
  • Show generation history and restore service state across launches
  • Send push notifications you request
  • Detect abuse, fraud, outages, and service errors
  • Measure product usage and improve reliability, UX, pricing, and model selection

3. When We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only as needed to operate the service, including with:

  • AI and storage providers: Inputs such as prompts, images, audio, and videos may be sent to model and storage providers, including fal.ai-hosted services, to generate results and move files through the processing pipeline.
  • Supabase: We use Supabase for backend data storage, generation records, credit operations, and analytics events.
  • Apple and Adapty: We use these services for in-app purchase handling, subscription state, and purchase validation.
  • Service providers and legal requests: We may disclose information if needed to enforce our terms, protect the service, or comply with law.

4. Storage and Retention

Generated media that you save in the app is downloaded to your device. The service also keeps backend records related to generations, credits, purchases, analytics, and account state.

Prompt history and saved creations may remain on your device until you remove them. Server-side records are retained for operational, security, accounting, support, and legal purposes for as long as reasonably necessary.

Files routed through third-party storage or model providers may also be retained under those providers' operational practices.

5. Your Choices and Controls

  • You can clear local history and saved media from within the app.
  • You can use the app's reset/delete flow to clear local app state and create a fresh local identity on that device.
  • The in-app reset flow does not by itself guarantee deletion of all server-side records associated with a prior anonymous account. For server-side deletion requests, contact us through the support page.
  • You can disable notifications in iOS Settings at any time.

6. Tracking and Advertising

We do not use the app to track you across third-party apps or websites for targeted advertising. The app does not currently rely on App Tracking Transparency for its analytics flow.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. This includes transport encryption, Keychain storage for sensitive local tokens, and service-side access controls. No system is perfectly secure, and you should avoid uploading content you are not comfortable transmitting to third-party processors.

8. Children's Privacy

Diffuria AI is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the effective date above and, where appropriate, provide notice inside the app.

10. Contact

If you have privacy questions or want to request deletion of server-side records tied to your account, use our support page.
