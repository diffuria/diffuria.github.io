Frequently Asked Questions

How do credits work?

Each AI generation costs credits. Image generation costs 1–10 credits depending on the model. Video generation costs 3–40 credits. You earn free credits daily and can purchase more or subscribe for recurring credits.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → select Diffuria AI → Cancel Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

Why was my prompt blocked?

AI models include safety filters that block certain types of content. Try rephrasing your prompt with different wording.

How long does video generation take?

Most videos take 1–5 minutes. Premium models like Kling 3.0 can take up to 10 minutes. You'll receive a notification when your video is ready.

I was charged but didn't get credits

Go to Profile → Restore Purchases. If the issue persists, contact us using the form above.

Can I use generated content commercially?

Yes. You own the images and videos you create with Diffuria AI and can use them for personal or commercial purposes.
